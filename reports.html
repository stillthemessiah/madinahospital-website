Accessing Your Medical Reports & Records

Last Updated: July 17, 2025

At Madina Community Hospital & Specialist Services, we believe in empowering our patients with access to their health information. Your medical reports and records are vital for understanding your health journey and making informed decisions about your care. This page outlines the process for accessing your records.

Methods for Accessing Your Records

We offer several convenient ways to access your medical information:

1. Online Patient Portal (Coming Soon)

We are actively developing a secure online patient portal that will allow you to access a summary of your medical records, test results, appointment history, and more from the comfort of your home. Stay tuned for updates on its launch!

  • Secure access with personal login credentials.
  • View test results as soon as they are available.
  • Review your medication list and allergies.
  • Access summaries of your past visits.

2. In-Person Request

You can request a copy of your medical records by visiting our Medical Records Department in person. This is the fastest way to obtain physical copies of extensive records.

  • Location: Medical Records Department, Ground Floor (near Main Reception)
  • Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • What to Bring: Valid photo identification (e.g., National ID, Passport, Driver's License) is required to verify your identity.
  • Authorization: If you are requesting records for someone else, you must provide a valid power of attorney or legal guardianship documents.

3. Written Request (Mail/Email)

You can also submit a written request for your medical records via mail or email. Please allow sufficient time for processing.

  • Download Form: A "Release of Information" form is available for download on our Patient Resources page.
  • Required Information: The form requires your full name, date of birth, contact information, specific dates of service or types of records requested, and your signature.
  • Submission: Completed forms can be mailed to our hospital address or sent via email to the Medical Records Department (see contact details below).
  • Processing Time: Please allow 5-7 business days for your request to be processed once received.

Important Considerations

  • Fees: While basic access to your records is often free, a reasonable fee may apply for extensive paper copies or specialized requests. You will be informed of any fees in advance.
  • Privacy: We are committed to protecting your privacy. All requests for medical records are handled in strict accordance with patient confidentiality laws and regulations.
  • Understanding Your Records: If you have questions about the information in your medical records, please schedule an appointment with your physician to discuss them.

Need Assistance with Your Medical Records?

Our Medical Records Department is here to assist you with any questions or help you through the process of accessing your health information.
