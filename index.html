Frequently Asked Questions About Our Hospital

How can I book an appointment at Madina Community Hospital? ▼
You can book an appointment by calling our reception at +233 59 916 6781 or +233 24 920 6668, or by using our online booking system available on the "Book Appointment" section of our website.
What insurance plans do you accept at Madina Community Hospital? ▼
We accept a wide range of national and international insurance plans, including Private Insurance and NHIS. Please contact our billing department or check our Patient Resources section for a detailed list of accepted insurance providers.
What are the visiting hours for Madina Community Hospital? ▼
General visiting hours are from 10 AM to 8 PM daily. For specific departments or intensive care units, please inquire with the respective nursing station. Our OPD services are available 24/7 for your convenience.
Does Madina Community Hospital offer telehealth services? ▼
Yes, we offer virtual consultations for various specialties to provide convenient healthcare from your home. Please contact us to see if your condition is suitable for a telehealth appointment.
